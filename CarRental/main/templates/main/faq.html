Here are answers to common questions about your rental, payment, and account management.

Booking & Eligibility

You will need a valid driver's license (local or international), a government-issued ID (such as an Iqama or National ID), and a credit card in the renter's name for the security deposit.

The minimum age to rent a vehicle is typically 21 years old. Renters under 25 may be subject to an additional young driver fee, depending on the car group.

Payment & Fees

Basic third-party liability insurance is usually included. You have the option to purchase additional comprehensive coverage (CDW/Super CDW) during the online booking process for extra peace of mind.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex). For the mandatory security deposit, a credit card is required. Debit cards may be accepted for the rental amount but not for the deposit.

Pick-up & Return

Late returns may incur an hourly late fee or an additional full-day rental charge, depending on the delay. Please inform us immediately if you anticipate returning the vehicle later than the agreed time.
